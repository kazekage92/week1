Desa Park City

In 1999, ParkCity acquired the 473-acre Desa ParkCity site.
From a barren quarry mine, the site has become the cradle of one of Malaysia’s top selling communities.
Today, the multiple award-winning master-plan of Desa ParkCity is regarded by many as innovative, imaginative and successful model for a lively, safe, sustainable and healthy community.
